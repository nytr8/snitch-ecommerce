## Brand & Style
Modern Editorial Snitch is a high-end, minimalist design system tailored for a premium fashion brand. It prioritizes clarity, whitespace, and sharp typographic hierarchy to create a 'Quiet Luxury' atmosphere.

## Visual Language
- **Typography**: Uses 'Instrument Sans' for a stylish, modern headline feel and 'Inter' for clean, readable body copy.
- **Color**: A strictly limited palette of pure white, off-white (#FCFBFA), and deep black (#1A1A1A).
- **Geometry**: Subtle 8px rounding on buttons and cards to feel approachable yet architectural.
- **Space**: Generous margins and large gaps between sections to allow products to breathe.
